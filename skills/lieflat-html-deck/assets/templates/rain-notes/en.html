Stability and limits

note · one

The best tasks are not single-turn questions.
They have context, constraints,
and acceptance criteria.

Give it a target and material.
It becomes more like a working partner.

The more specific the brief,
the steadier the result.

note · two

It is not a fact machine.
Anything current, priced, regulated,
or research-heavy needs sources.

GPT-5.5 can drive progress.
Humans still own judgment.
That boundary matters.
