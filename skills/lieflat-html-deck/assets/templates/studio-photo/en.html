Fabric &
Construction
Fabric System

Three-layer construction: wind-resistant abrasion-grade outer, PU breathable membrane, soft-fleece backer. Total weight kept under 240g/m².

Thermal Tiers

An in-house three-level warmth system maps fill density to ambient temperature. No more guessing on grams.

Cut & Construction

Three-dimensional pattern frees range of motion while keeping the wind seal — engineered for the full commute-to-trail span.
