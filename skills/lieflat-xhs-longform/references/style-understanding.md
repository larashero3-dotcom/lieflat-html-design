# Lieflat XHS Longform Style Understanding

Longform cards are for reading and saving. They should not behave like covers, posters, or slide decks.

## Longform Structure

- One strong title.
- Optional short subtitle or verdict.
- One clear opening section.
- Continuous body copy with 1-3 sections.
- Compact metadata or footer.
- No dense appendix tables.
- No decorative screenshot slots unless they support the article.

## Do Not

- Do not turn every article into a scorecard.
- Do not keep demo badges if the article does not need them.
- Do not overfill the card; cut copy before shrinking body text too far.
- Do not use a cover-style title-only layout for longform reading.
